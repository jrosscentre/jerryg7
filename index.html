Relaxed Thinking

Our brains are constanlty thinking and being stimulated by the world around us, but our brain often gets in the way of its self. To get out of our own way, it is best for our brains to relax while being stimulated. This relaxation is not just sitting in a beach chair listening to Jimmy Buffet, but taking a removed approach to the situation and allow the brain to be stimulated as it activates naturally. This strategy can allow the brain to open up to different information and pathways that could not be reached if the brain is forcably stimulated i.e. thinking too hard in uneccessary fashion.
